# Privacy Policy for HelloWorld

*Last updated: January 2026*

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **HelloWorld**. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect
When you use our app, we may collect the following types of information:

### A. Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: If you choose to log in using **Facebook** or **Google**, we collect your basic profile information such as your name, email address, and profile picture to create your account and personalize your experience.
- **User Content**: Any data you enter into the app (e.g., wedding details, guest lists, expenses) is stored securely to provide the app's core functionality.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
- Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
- Personalize your experience within the app.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use third-party services to help operate our app:
- **Facebook Login**: Used for authentication. We only request public profile and email permissions.
- **Google Sign-In**: Used for authentication.

## 5. Data Deletion
You have the right to request the deletion of your data.
- **In-App Deletion**: You can delete your account directly within the app settings (Profile -> Delete Account). This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.
- **Manual Request**: If you are unable to access the app, you may contact us to request data deletion.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
